Our Services

At SAFEGATE DXB Cargo, we specialize in providing fast, reliable, and affordable cargo solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you are shipping personal items, commercial goods, or sensitive materials, we ensure every shipment is handled with care and delivered on time. Cargo Destinations & Origin Countries We connect businesses and individuals across the globe with East Africa. Our main origin countries include Dubai, China, India, Turkey, USA, Thailand, Canada, and Saudi Arabia. No matter where your goods come from, SAFEGATE DXB Cargo ensures safe and seamless delivery to major cities in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Somalia, including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Dar es Salaam, Kampala, and Mogadishu.
